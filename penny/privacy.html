Privacy Policy

Penny — Personal Expense Tracker

Last updated: June 7, 2026

Penny ("the app", "we", "us") is a personal finance app that helps you track expenses, income, budgets, and loans. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. We built Penny to keep your financial data private and under your control.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: your email address, used to create and sign in to your account.
  • Financial data you enter: transactions (amount, merchant, category, note, date), budgets, and loan/payment records.
  • Preferences: settings such as your preferred currency and onboarding status.

We do not collect your location, contacts, photos, or device microphone audio. Penny has no voice or ad-tracking features.

How We Use Your Data

  • To display your balances, charts, and spending insights.
  • To sync your data across your own devices through your account.
  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers.

Where Your Data Is Stored

Your data is stored in a private database hosted on Supabase, isolated to your account. Access is protected by row-level security so that only you, signed in with your credentials, can read or write your records.

Data Sharing

We share data only with the infrastructure provider needed to run the app (Supabase, for authentication and database hosting). We may disclose data if required by law. We never share it for marketing.

Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is kept as long as your account exists. You can delete individual transactions and records at any time from within the app. To delete your entire account and all associated data, contact us at the email below and we will remove it.

Your Rights

  • Access and export your transactions (CSV export is built into the app).
  • Correct or delete any record you have entered.
  • Request full account deletion.

Children's Privacy

Penny is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be noted by updating the "Last updated" date above.

Penny stores your data in your private account. Only you can read it. Your financial information stays yours.
