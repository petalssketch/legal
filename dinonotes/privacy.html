Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

1. Introduction

DinoNotes ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the DinoNotes mobile application ("App").

By using DinoNotes, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture (if provided). Authentication is handled securely through our backend provider.

User-Generated Content: This includes notes you create through typing, voice recording, document scanning, AI Agent research, HiLift extraction, and Smart Summary features. Your content is stored securely in our database to enable access across sessions.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the App, including features used, credit consumption, and general usage patterns. This helps us improve the App and maintain fair credit billing.

Voice Data: When you use the Voice Notes feature, audio recordings are temporarily processed through a third-party transcription service (OpenAI Whisper) to convert speech to text. Audio data is not permanently stored after transcription is complete.

Image Data: When you use the Scan or HiLift features, images are temporarily uploaded for AI-powered text extraction (via Google Gemini). Images are deleted from our storage after processing is complete.

Payment Information: Credit purchases are processed through Google Play's in-app purchase system and our payment partner (RevenueCat). We do not directly collect or store your payment card details. We only record the credit amount associated with your account.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App's features and functionality
  • Process and deliver AI-powered features including voice transcription, document scanning, OCR, AI Agent research, HiLift extraction, and Smart Summary
  • Manage your account, credit balance, and purchase history
  • Enable sharing and real-time collaboration on boards with other users
  • Send notifications related to board sharing and collaboration activity
  • Respond to support requests and communicate with you about the App
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or security issues

4. Third-Party Services

DinoNotes integrates with the following third-party services to deliver its features:

  • Supabase — Secure database, authentication, and file storage
  • OpenAI (Whisper) — Voice-to-text transcription for Voice Notes
  • OpenAI (GPT) — Text generation for AI Agent Notes and summarization
  • Google Gemini — OCR and text extraction for Scan Notes, HiLift, and PDF summarization
  • Tavily — Web search and content extraction for AI Agent Notes and webpage summarization
  • RevenueCat — In-app purchase management and credit fulfillment
  • Google Play Billing — Payment processing for credit purchases

Each third-party service operates under its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review their policies. We only share the minimum data necessary for each service to function.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard infrastructure provided by Supabase. We implement the following security measures:

  • Encrypted data transmission using HTTPS/TLS
  • Secure authentication with token-based access control
  • Row-level security policies to ensure users can only access their own data
  • Temporary storage of media files (images and audio), which are deleted after processing

While we take reasonable measures to protect your data, no system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. DinoNotes is ad-free.

We may share your data only in the following circumstances:

  • Collaboration: When you share a board with another user, the content of that board becomes accessible to the recipient
  • Service Providers: With third-party services listed above, solely to provide App functionality
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, regulation, or legal process
  • Safety: To protect the rights, safety, or property of DinoNotes, our users, or others

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export your notes and data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at petalssketch@yahoo.com.

8. Data Retention

We retain your account data and notes for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

Temporary data such as uploaded images and audio recordings are deleted immediately after processing.

9. Children's Privacy

DinoNotes is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
